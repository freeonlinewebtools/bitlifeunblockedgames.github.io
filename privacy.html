Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

Bitlife Unblocked Games is designed for lightweight browser gameplay. We keep data collection minimal and prioritize local, on-device storage for convenience features.

What data is stored

  • Favorites: Saved in browser localStorage so your favorite games persist between visits.
  • Recently played: Stored locally to help you quickly return to games.

Third-party content

Games are loaded from external static hosts. Those hosts may have separate logs or policies outside MK2 Arcade control.

Advertising and analytics

If enabled, ad providers may use cookies and identifiers according to their own policies. You can manage ad personalization in your provider settings.

Your control

You can clear local favorites and recent history at any time by clearing browser site data for this domain.

Contact

For privacy concerns, contact the site maintainer through the project repository.
